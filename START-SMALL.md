# Evolving - Quick Start (5 Minutes)

## Welcome!

Evolving is your **second brain** - an AI-powered system that:
- Captures your ideas and intelligently connects them
- Stores knowledge and helps you find it again
- Thinks together with you (Sparring)

---

## First Steps

### 1. Set up your profile
Open `_ONBOARDING.md`, fill in the basics, then say:
```
Process the onboarding
```

### 2. Capture your first idea
Simply say:
```
I have an idea: [your idea]
```
Or use `/idea-new`

### 3. Explore the system
```
Show me my ideas
```
Or use `/idea-list`

---

## Your 3 Most Important Commands

| Command | What it does |
|---------|-------------|
| `/idea-new` | Capture new idea with AI analysis |
| `/knowledge-add` | Save knowledge (learnings, tips) |
| `/sparring` | Free brainstorming & thinking |
